# Whisper of the Void

Whisper of the Void is a specialized client-side psychological horror modification for Minecraft. It does not add new blocks, items, or monsters. Instead, it focuses on the inherent loneliness of the game, transforming it into a persistent state of unease.

## Concept

Minecraft has always carried a subtle sense of isolation, especially deep underground or in the vast, empty reaches of a world. This mod amplifies that atmosphere by introducing a sophisticated paranoia system that studies your behavior and surroundings.

Everything you experience is localized entirely within your game client. Because the mod does not send data to the server, it is compatible with any multiplayer environment, from massive public networks to private vanilla servers. To other players, you are simply playing the game normally. To you, the game is no longer what it seems.

## The Paranoia Engine

The core of the experience is an invisible tracking system. The engine monitors several factors, including:

*   Total time spent in the current session.
*   Depth (Y-level).
*   Light levels in your immediate vicinity.
  
As your paranoia level rises, the world begins to lose its consistency. The mod utilizes advanced techniques to distort your perception of audio, visual rendering, and the game’s user interface.

## Trust Nothing

The goal of Whisper of the Void is to make you doubt your own senses and your understanding of the game’s mechanics. When you can no longer trust what you see in the darkness, what you hear behind you, or even what the system menus are telling you, the true experience begins.

If you suspect the game is malfunctioning or displaying impossible information, the mod is likely functioning exactly as intended.

## Technical Details

*   Platform: Fabric Loader
*   Versions: Compatible with Minecraft 1.21 through 1.21.6
*   Environment: 100% Client-Side
*   Safety: The mod does not manipulate server-side data, delete real items, or interfere with world save integrity.

## Installation

1. Ensure you have the Fabric Loader installed for a compatible version of Minecraft.
2. Place the Fabric API jar into your mods folder.
3. Place the Whisper of the Void jar into your mods folder.

4. Launch the game.

## Disclaimer

This modification uses psychological pressure, sensory distortion, and auditory discomfort. It is intended for players who enjoy a high-tension atmosphere and the feeling of being watched. 

If the void looks back, it is recommended to find the light. Assuming the light is still there.
